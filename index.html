Installation Steps

  1. Open your Coda workspace
  2. Navigate to Packs in the left sidebar
  3. Search for "Document Generation"
  4. Click "Add to doc" to install
  5. Authorize Google Drive/Docs access when prompted

Create Your First Document

Step 1: Prepare your template with placeholders:

Dear <<ClientName>>, Your invoice #<<InvoiceNumber>> for $<<Amount>> is due on <<DueDate>>. Best regards, <<YourName>>

Step 2: Use the CreateDocument formula in Coda:

=CreateDocument( "your-template-url", "parent-folder-id", "2024/Invoices", "Invoice-<<InvoiceNumber>>", "", "ClientName", "ABC Corp", "InvoiceNumber", "001", "Amount", "1500", "DueDate", "2024-02-15", "YourName", "John Smith" )
